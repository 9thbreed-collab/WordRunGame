## Content Filters

### Difficulty (1-5 scale)
- **1-2:** Short words (3-5 chars), one obvious answer
- **3:** Medium words (5-7 chars), one obvious answer
- **4:** Longer words (6-8 chars), 1-2 plausible answers
- **5:** Any length, 2-3 plausible answers (second half only)

### Rarity
- **Common:** Everyday phrases (sunflower, butterfly, bedroom)
- **Familiar:** Recognizable but less frequent (clipboard, bankroll)
- **Uncommon:** Valid but rarely used (topsoil, hatband)
- **Rare:** Obscure compounds (second half only)

### Lore Tags
- Themed words diluted with neutral words
- Not every word matches land theme
- Detailed lore docs coming later

## Repetition Guidelines

- Repeated pairs/words: Acceptable if spread out
- Same word in different pairs: OK across lands
- Same pair twice: Avoid within same nation
- High-frequency words (ball, house, man): Max 1 per land
